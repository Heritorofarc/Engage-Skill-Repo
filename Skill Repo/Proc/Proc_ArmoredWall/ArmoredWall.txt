Armored Wall

[MSID_H_ArmoredWall]
If unit's HP is 25% or more at start of combat, 
adds +25% to unit and costs foe -20% to trigger 
rate for all skills with trigger rates. Unit 
recovers 7 HP after combat. If engaged, for 
first attack targeting unit during current phase, 
prevents 40% of damage taken.
